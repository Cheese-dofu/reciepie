How to make Japaneese curry

Why Japaneese curry?
    Ingredients for the curry
  • Onions (2 large)
  • Yukon gold potatoes (3)
  • Carrots (2)
  • Ginger (1 tsp, grated)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Chicken thighs (1.5 lb, boneless skinless)
  • Black pepper
  • apples
  • oil (1½ Tbsp)
  • Chicken stock (4 cups)
  • Soy sauce (1 Tbsp)
  • Japanese curry roux (7 to 8 oz / 200 to 230 g)
      • the recepie
  • Cut onions into wedges. Cut carrots into rolling wedges. Cut potatoes into quarters and soak them in water for 15 min.
  • Grate ginger and apple. Mince garlic.
  • Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with pepper.
  • Heat oil in a pot, cook onions until soft. Add garlic and ginger. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.

How to make Japaneese curry The orginal recepie link

    Ingredients for the curry

  1. Onions (2 large)
  2. Yukon gold potatoes (3)
  3. Carrots (2)
  4. Ginger (1 tsp, grated)
  5. Garlic (2 cloves)
  6. Chicken thighs (1.5 lb, boneless skinless)
  7. Black pepper
  8. apples
  9. oil (1½ Tbsp)
  10. Chicken stock (4 cups)
  11. Soy sauce (1 Tbsp)
  12. Japanese curry roux (7 to 8 oz / 200 to 230 g)

    the recepie

  1. Cut onions into wedges. Cut carrots into rolling wedges. Cut potatoes into quarters and soak them in water for 15 min.
  2. Grate ginger and apple. Mince garlic.
  3. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with pepper.
  4. Heat oil in a pot, cook onions until soft. Add garlic and ginger. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  5. Add broth, apple, honey, soy sauce, ketchup, carrots, and potatoes. Simmer covered until vegetables are tender (about 15–20 min). Skim foam if needed.
  6. Turn off heat, dissolve curry roux into the pot. Turn heat back on and simmer until thick (5–10 min).
  7. Serves with rice and pickles
  8. -NOTE: You can add a lot more things like Honey, Mirin, Japanese BBQ sauce, and more to taste. Also serve with rice
